Usage

Use Tone Analyzer to detect three types of tones from written text: emotions, social tendencies, and language style. Emotions identified include things like anger, cheerfulness and sadness. Identified social tendencies include things from the Big Five personality traits used by some psychologists. These include openness, conscientiousness, extraversion, agreeableness, and neuroticism. Identified language styles include things like confident, analytical, and tentative. Input email and other written media into the Tone Analyzer service, and use the results to determine if your writing comes across with the tone, personality traits, and writing style that you want for your intended audience.

Analyze tone

Analyzes the tone of a piece of text. The message is analyzed for several tones - social, emotional, and language. For each tone, various traits are derived. For example, conscientiousness, agreeableness, and openness.
